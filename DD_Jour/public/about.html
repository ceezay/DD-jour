About Us

DD Jour brings about revolutionary customized all encompassing services for the modern home owner. The brainchild of two friends turned business partners DD Jour is managed by Mr.Himanshu Das and Mr.Prakhar. Prakhar and Himanshu combine decades of Design & Marketing expertise with a shared dedication to creating comfortable and sophisticated interiors. We have worked hard to create our efficient and experienced team of interior designers, technicians and administrators and we constantly strive to provide our clients with the best professional services through our collective rich experience, creativity and design knowledge. When we take up a project, it is all encompassing. We give you end-to-end solutions through our expansive network of preferred design professionals. If a project needs an architect, landscape architect or any other specialist, and you don’t have your own, we can recommend professionals with whom we have developed strong collaborative working relationships. While Prakahr’s energy is directed towards the Sales and Marketing and scaling new heights for the company, Himanshu has rich experience in the field of Interiors along with his post graduation degree from NIFT in Fashion & Lifestyle and Interior Design Accessories.

With each project whether it is residential or commercial interiors, the team brings to the table something distinct and unique with the sole aim of building an aesthetically pleasing environment for the people using that space. Having worked with huge names like Future group - Evoke, & Tesco, DD Jour’s rich experience and insight is thoroughly visible in each of the projects. We at DD Jour totally love the phrase “Feel at Home!” When we started out we decided to incorporate this line in all our projects. We understand the sentiment behind each homeowner and work tirelessly to ensure that your tastes, aesthetics and choices meet with our creative, functional and optimum efficiency to create delightful interiors for you and your family to enjoy and delight you each day. Over the years DD Jour has acquired a deliberate bespoke approach to envisioning the finest, unique and exceptional interior designs and then assessing how the elements can be worked optimally for the people who would be using those spaces.
